ABOUT THIS EVENT

Gebeya IT Academy is hosting a Software Developer Conference in Ethiopia in late February. All software and application developers, IT professionals, techies, startups technopreneurs and computer science students are invited to attend and to share knowledge about new breakthrough technologies. The conference will be held on Saturday, February 25 and Sunday, February 26 at the African Union headquarter in Addis Ababa. The goal of the conference is to showcase the many opportunities that exist in Ethiopia’s growing tech and enterpreneur space. Turing the conference, we will have workshops, demos, trainings and interventions from both local technology company leaders and multinational technology companies. The following topics will be covered: Mobile, Security, Cloud, API Economy, Internet of Things (IoT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Drone Tech, FinTech, Wireless Tech, Cryptography, Networks, Big Data, Enterprise Architecture, Integrated systems and etc.

It is an online workplace for Africa that matches highly skilled, certified and multilingual IT professionals with customers to develop innovative technology solutions, improving development efficiency, scalability and mobile success. Gebeya’ s vision is to become the premier software engineering and IT training academy in Africa, while becoming the number one global IT services workplace that matches African IT talent with clients across Africa and the rest of the world. Our mission is to produce African IT talent and provide an online IT services workplace for the global business market, expanding IT capabilities with training; increasing availability of IT talent, reducing IT operational costs and eliminating technology hardware and software needs for our clients; and increasing opportunities for our IT professionals.
